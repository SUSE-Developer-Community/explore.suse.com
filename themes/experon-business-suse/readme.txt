== Description ==

Experon Business SUSE is the modified version of the free Experon Business theme.
The original Experon Business theme is developed by Think Up Themes Ltd.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Themes and click the 'Add New' button.
2. Type in Experon Business in the search form and press the 'Enter' key on your keyboard.
3. Click on the 'Activate' button to use your new theme right away.
4. Go to Appearance - About Experon Business in the admin area of your website for a guide on how to customize this theme.
5. Navigate to Appearance > Customize in your admin panel and customize to taste.
